We are happy to announce that we have opened the nomination window for the **IEEE-RAS Technical Committee on Model-Based Optimization for Robotics 2025 Best Paper Award**, which will come with a $500 travel award for the winner! Eligible papers for this year's award will have appeared at a conference or in a journal in 2025 (online or in print). We encourage TC members and community members to nominate their favorite papers of the past year (including their own!).

**Nominations Due**: February 7th, 2026 *[self-nominations encouraged, but limited to 2 per group]*

**Winners Announced**: March 2026 (targeted)

**Nomination Form**: [forms.gle/8cEMngctzYU25E5A6](forms.gle/8cEMngctzYU25E5A6)

Full policies and procedures for the best paper award can be found [here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1dhywlHwxx6VVKmtMkk4czRZ1vb4_ik3FIAM7NcpYvcs).
