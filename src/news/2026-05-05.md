# 2025 Best Paper Award Finalists and Winner
We are happy to announce the winners and finalists of this year's Best paper award!

🏆 **Winner**:

- **Sensitivity-Aware Model Predictive Control for Robots with Parametric Uncertainty**, by *T. Belvedere, M. Cognetti, G. Oriolo, P. R. Giordano*

⭐**Finalists**:

 - **Max Entropy Moment Kalman Filter for Polynomial Systems with Arbitrary Noise**, by *S. Teng, H. Zhang, D. Jin, A. Jasour, R. Vasudevan, M. Ghaffari, L. Carlone*
 - **Chance-Constrained Convex MPC for Robust Quadruped Locomotion Under Parametric and Additive Uncertainties**, by *A. Trivedi, S. Prajapati, M. Zolotas, M. Everett, T. Padır*

Congratulations to all the authors! The winner is awarded a 500$ travel award.
