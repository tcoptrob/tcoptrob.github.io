# Call for Student Representatives

We are seeking **Student Representatives** to work closely with the **Co-Chairs** and **Associate Co-Chairs** of the Technical Committee on Model-based Optimization for Robotics.

## Responsibilities

As a Student Representative, your responsibilities will include:

1. Assisting in the planning and execution of committee events (e.g., meetings, workshops)  
2. Facilitating communication between committee members and students in the field  
3. Contributing to the development of committee initiatives  
4. Promoting the committee's work and recruiting new student members  
5. Representing the student perspective within the committee  
6. Developing the **ICRA Welcome Kit** for the OptRob TC  

---

## Requirements

Applicants must meet the following requirements:

1. Enrollment in a **PhD or MS-PhD program** related to model-based optimization until the end of the term  
2. Membership in **IEEE** and the **IEEE Robotics & Automation Society (RAS)**  
3. Demonstrated interest in model-based optimization for robotics  
4. Excellent communication and organizational skills  
5. Ability to commit to the role and volunteer for **one year**

---

## Benefits

Benefits of serving as a Student Representative include:

1. Networking opportunities with leading professionals and researchers in the field  
2. Leadership experience and active contribution to the community  
3. Enhanced understanding of both technical and practical aspects of model-based optimization for robotics  
4. Participation in a global community promoting model-based optimization methods  
5. Support to attend **RAS flagship conferences** to assist with event and workshop organization  
6. **One free registration** for RAS-sponsored conferences (e.g., ICRA, IROS, RoboSoft) if assisting with Welcome Kit production
